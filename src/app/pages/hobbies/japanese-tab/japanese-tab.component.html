My Japanese Adventure /
僕の日本語冒険

Between 2007 and 2013, I embarked on a self-directed study of the Japanese language, culminating in passing the JLPT N5 and N4 exams. My passion for Japanese culture led me to choose it as a minor when I enrolled at Binghamton University. Through dedicated coursework, I further honed my language skills and successfully passed the JLPT N3 exam in December 2015. The intricate beauty of the Japanese language and the richness of its culture have been a long-standing passion of mine, deeply influencing my academic and personal pursuits.
