Japanese and Me

From 2007 until 2013, I was self-taught in Japanese and passed the JLPT N5 and N4 exams. Once I started college, I chose Japanese as a minor. In December of 2015, I passed the JLPT N3 exams as part of a class I took. The Japanese language and Culture has been a long time passion of mine.

2011 Trip To Japan

I've been to Japan once and it was an incredible adventure. I need to go back! I traveled to Tokyo, Kyoto, and Hiroshima. Below you'll find various photos from all 3 prefectures. I got to ride Shinkansen (Bullet Trains), see temples, and enjoy ordering Starbucks in Japanese!
